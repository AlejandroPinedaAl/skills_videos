# TikTok Native Hook — Ad Creative Rules for TikTok

## Core Principle
TikTok's algorithm rewards content that feels organic. Ads that look like ads get scrolled past. Ads that look like authentic creator content get watched, saved, and convert.

**Rule #1:** If it looks like a TV commercial, it will die in 1.5 seconds.

---

## Visual Rules (Non-Negotiable)

### Frame Composition
- **Subject position:** center-left of frame (right side is where TikTok UI sits — avoid it)
- **Safe zones:** keep key visuals between 15% from top and 20% from bottom
- **Avoid bottom 20%:** that's where the caption, username, and song overlay live
- **Avoid right 10%:** that's the like/comment/share button column
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 ONLY. Never 1:1 or 16:9 on TikTok.

### Aesthetic
- **Phone quality is fine** — slightly imperfect video performs better than over-produced
- Natural lighting > studio lighting for UGC formats
- Real locations > white studio backgrounds
- One person speaking directly to camera > narrator + b-roll
- Handheld or slight shake = authentic. Locked tripod shot = corporate

### Text Overlays
- Large, bold captions — minimum 32pt equivalent
- Use TikTok-style text (not branded lower-thirds)
- Captions are mandatory: 85% of TikTok is watched without sound
- Key phrase on screen within first 2 seconds

## Timing Structure (9–15 seconds for conversion ads)

```
0 – 1.5s  | PATTERN INTERRUPT
           | Something visually OR verbally unexpected
           | Must break scroll behavior instantly
           | Examples: sudden movement, surprising statement, bold text pop

1.5 – 3s  | PROBLEM / CURIOSITY
           | Name the pain or raise the question
           | This is where you earn the next 10 seconds

3 – 7s    | DEMO / MECHANISM / "THIS IS WHY IT WORKS"
           | Show the product, the result, the process
           | Or deliver the insight that reframes the problem
           | Keep cuts fast: 1–1.5 seconds per shot max

7 – 11s   | MAIN BENEFIT
           | One clear outcome — no lists, no multiple benefits
           | Make it concrete and specific to the viewer

11 – 15s  | CTA
           | One action only
           | Verbal + on-screen text simultaneously
           | For TikTok: "Escríbenos por DM", "Link en bio", "Comenta [word]"
```

## Extended Format (21–34 seconds for awareness/consideration)
```
0 – 2s    | Pattern interrupt
2 – 5s    | Problem/setup
5 – 12s   | Story or demo (the middle third)
12 – 20s  | Proof (result, testimonial clip, stat)
20 – 28s  | Solution framing
28 – 34s  | CTA + urgency
```

---

## Hook Delivery Styles for TikTok

### Verbal Hook (person speaking)
- First word must be strong: "Stop," "Wait," "This," "If you," "Nobody tells you"
- Speak fast — TikTok pacing is 1.5–2x normal conversation speed
- Look directly into camera
- No "Hi guys, today I want to tell you about…" — start mid-thought

### Visual Hook (no speech)
- Unexpected action in first frame
- Before/after reveal starting with the "after"
- Close-up of a surprising result
- Text shock: a stat or claim that stops the scroll

### Combined (best for conversion)
- Bold text on screen + person reacting to it
- Product in hand being used + voiceover naming the problem it solves

---

## What Kills TikTok Ads
- Logo in first 3 seconds
- Slow zoom-in or fade-in opener
- Professional voiceover (sounds like radio)
- Stock footage
- More than 2 different CTAs
- Horizontal video letterboxed to 9:16

---

## CTA Options by Objective
| Objective | CTA |
|---|---|
| Lead generation | "Escríbenos 'INFO' por DM" / "Comenta 'QUIERO'" |
| Website traffic | "Link en bio" / "Visita [brand].com" |
| App install | "Descárgala gratis, link en bio" |
| Store visit | "Encuéntranos en [platform]" |
| Awareness | "Síguenos para más" |

## Platform-Specific Output Format
When generating TikTok scripts, always include:
- Duration: `[X]s`
- Hook type: `[verbal/visual/combined]`
- Pattern interrupt description
- On-screen text at each key moment
- Caption safe zone note
- CTA exact wording
