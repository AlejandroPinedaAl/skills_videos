# Star-Story-Solution UGC — Testimonial Video Framework

## Framework Logic
People trust people. This framework wraps the sales message inside a relatable human story. The "Star" is not the product — it's a specific person the viewer can identify with. The story creates emotional investment. The solution (product) appears as the natural resolution to a real problem.

**Key principle:** The less it feels like an ad, the better it converts. Every word should sound like something a real customer would actually say.

---

## Structure (15-second version)

```
STAR       0 – 3s
├── Introduce a specific, relatable person (not "everyone")
├── Give them a context that the target audience immediately recognizes
├── First person is strongest: "Yo soy…" / "Como dueño de…"
└── Examples:
    "Soy dueña de una clínica de estética en Medellín."
    "Llevaba 2 años intentando crecer mi negocio con redes sociales."
    "Gasto $500 al mes en publicidad y no sabía si funcionaba."

STORY      3 – 9s
├── The specific conflict or frustration — one clear problem
├── Name what was tried before and why it failed
├── Keep it conversational — real people don't use marketing language
└── Examples:
    "Publicaba todos los días, contrataba diseñadores, pero los mensajes no llegaban."
    "Probé con una agencia y me cobraban una fortuna sin resultados claros."
    "Siempre sentía que estaba adivinando qué anuncio iba a funcionar."

SOLUTION   9 – 13s
├── How they discovered the product/service
├── ONE specific thing it did that solved the problem
├── Don't list features — describe a moment of realization
└── Examples:
    "Con SaleADS, en una semana ya tenía campañas funcionando solas."
    "Por primera vez vi exactamente dónde iba cada peso y qué estaba convirtiendo."
    "El sistema genera los anuncios por mí — yo solo apruebo y publico."

CTA        13 – 15s
├── Natural recommendation, not a forced pitch
└── Examples:
    "Si tienes un negocio y quieres que tus ads funcionen de verdad, pruébalo."
    "Te lo recomiendo — y el primero es gratis."
```

---

## Structure (30-second version)

```
STAR       0 – 5s     Richer context, establishes credibility
STORY      5 – 15s    Two-beat conflict: what they tried + why it failed
SOLUTION   15 – 25s   Discovery moment + specific result with numbers
CTA        25 – 30s   Warm recommendation + call to action
```

---

## Star Profiles by Business Type

### For Service Businesses (SaleADS use case)
- "Dueño/a de negocio que gasta en publicidad sin ver resultados"
- "Emprendedor/a que intenta todo y no escala"
- "Encargado de marketing que no puede justificar el presupuesto"

### For Physical Products
- "La persona que tenía [specific problem the product solves]"
- Focus on relatable life context, not demographics

### For B2B / SaaS
- "Gerente / fundador que perdía tiempo en [manual process]"
- Use job title if it adds credibility

---

## Authenticity Rules
These make the script feel real (not scripted):

1. **No adjectives for the product in the story section** — no "amazing," "incredible," "game-changing" in the mouth of the testimonial person
2. **Use hesitation language:** "la verdad es que…", "no lo esperaba pero…", "fue cuando me di cuenta de que…"
3. **Name a specific detail** — not "gasté mucho" but "gasté $400 ese mes"
4. **Imperfect delivery > polished delivery** — slight pauses and natural speech patterns build trust

## Visual Direction

| Segment | Camera | Background | On-Screen Text |
|---|---|---|---|
| STAR | Eye-level, slight tilt | Real environment (office, home, store) | Name + role (optional) |
| STORY | Same or slight push-in | Same | Key pain phrase |
| SOLUTION | Same + possible B-roll cutaway | Real or dashboard screen | Key result metric |
| CTA | Pull back slightly | Same | Product name + action |

## Script Output Template

```
[TITLE]: SSS UGC – [Business Name] – [Platform] – [Duration]s

STAR (0-Xs): "[who they are — specific and relatable]"
DELIVERY: first person, direct to camera
ON-SCREEN: [name/role if relevant]

STORY (X-Xs): "[the specific conflict]"
DELIVERY: conversational, slightly frustrated tone
ON-SCREEN: [key pain phrase]
B-ROLL: [optional visual of the problem]

SOLUTION (X-Xs): "[discovery + one specific result]"
DELIVERY: realization tone — genuine surprise or relief
ON-SCREEN: [result metric or key phrase]
B-ROLL: [product/dashboard/result]

CTA (X-Xs): "[warm recommendation]"
ON-SCREEN: [product name + action]
BUTTON: "[CTA button]"
```
